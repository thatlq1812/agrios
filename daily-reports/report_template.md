## Tips for Writing Daily Reports

1. **Be Specific**: Use concrete numbers, file names, or technical terms
2. **Show Learning**: Highlight what you learned, not just what you did
3. **Be Honest**: Mention difficulties - it shows self-awareness
4. **Look Ahead**: Always include next steps to show proactivity
5. **Keep it Short**: Use bullet points, aim for 1-2 minutes reading time
6. **Use Technical Terms**: Demonstrate understanding of the domain
